See it: [Canvas Arts](https://artscanvas.org/)

**The goal:** PBS NewsHour sought a centralized online destination to showcase its in-depth arts and culture reporting. The site needed to be visually engaging, easy to navigate, and reflect the journalistic integrity of the NewsHour brand.

**The solution: a tailored WordPress experience**

I transformed a standard WordPress installation into the vibrant and informative Canvas website. Key contributions include:

- **Design:** Utilizing SCSS, I developed a custom theme that aligns with PBS NewsHour's aesthetic while prioritizing clean design and highlighting captivating arts-focused imagery.
- **Content organization:** Implemented a logical content structure and intuitive navigation. This allows users to effortlessly explore articles, videos, and featured series within the vast arts coverage of PBS NewsHour.
- **Dynamic elements:** I used JavaScript to add subtle but impactful features. Users enjoy smooth content loading, visually appealing interactions, and a streamlined media viewing experience.

**Project impact:** Canvas elevates PBS NewsHour's arts journalism with a compelling online presence. It provides a user-friendly hub for audiences to access, explore, and engage with high-quality reporting on all facets of arts and culture.
