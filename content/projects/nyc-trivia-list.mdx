**The problem:** Trivia buffs across New York City faced a frustrating challenge: finding up-to-date, comprehensive information on trivia nights throughout the city. Existing listings were often incomplete, outdated, or scattered across unreliable sources.

**The solution:**

I created NYC Trivia List to centralize and streamline the process of finding the perfect trivia night.

- **Robust backend:** The site runs on Next.js for reliable performance, integrated with Sanity CMS for straightforward content management.
- **Focus on user experience:** TailwindCSS ensures a stylish, responsive design tailored to finding trivia quickly. Day, borough, and neighborhood filters alongside an interactive Leaflet map help users pinpoint their ideal trivia spot in just a few clicks.
- **Automated updates:** Custom Python cron jobs proactively crawl trivia hosts' websites, seeking changes to schedules and venues.
- **Community driven:** A contact form enables site visitors to submit corrections and suggest new venues.

**Technical highlights**

- **Next.js expertise:** Delivered a fast, SEO-friendly, and easily maintainable web application.
- **CMS integration:** Demonstrated fluency in integrating Sanity CMS for non-technical content management.
- **Mapping solutions:** Seamlessly incorporated Leaflet for interactive map displays.
- **Proactive automation:** Used Python cron jobs to reduce manual tasks and ensure information stays reliable.

**Project impact:** NYC Trivia List makes it a breeze for New Yorkers to find fun, brain-tickling trivia nights across the city.
